about us

welcome to global trade coconut indonesia

Our coconut charcoal briquette company proudly serves as a leading supplier and exporter of premium coconut shell charcoal and coconut charcoal briquettes. With our base in Indonesia, we proudly hold a pivotal position within the industry. Our specialization in offering a diverse range of products, encompassing a variety of coconut shell charcoal types and two main types of charcoal briquettes meticulously tailored to cater to Shisha and Barbecue needs, each designed to meet various grades and specifications.

We are committed to delivering the highest quality products from raw materials, processes, production to packaging for a sustainable business and long term relationship.
